How to Import Your Signature

  1. Click the gear icon in Gmail and select "See all settings"
  2. Scroll down to the "Signature" section
  3. Click "Create new" and name your signature
  4. Click in the signature text box and paste (Ctrl+V or Cmd+V)
  5. Click "Save Changes" at the bottom
  1. Go to File > Options > Mail > Signatures
  2. Click "New" to create a new signature
  3. Paste your signature in the edit box (Ctrl+V)
  4. Set it as default for new messages and/or replies
  5. Click "OK" to save
  1. Open Mail and go to Mail > Settings (or Preferences)
  2. Click the "Signatures" tab
  3. Click the + button to add a new signature
  4. Paste your signature in the preview pane (Cmd+V)
  5. Drag the signature to the email account you want to use it with

Tip: Make sure to paste as rich text (not plain text) to preserve formatting.
